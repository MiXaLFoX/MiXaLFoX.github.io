about us

L.I.N.K. Global is an international network of independent forwarders, together taking care of millions of pounds of cargo transported over sea, in the air and via the road. The network was officially founded in 1994 by various members (founding members) and became a globally acknowledged and recognized organization through the years.

L.I.N.K. Global partners currently work in some 241 offices located in just over 60 countries worldwide, offering their customers global advantages and a local, personal touch. All L.I.N.K. Global partners and its employees share a passion for transport, know-how about their industry and in-depth knowledge of customs matters, legislation and global transportation developments.

Only one member per country.
