get a quote

We will be most happy to send you a quote for your shipment(s) after submitting information by using CARGO INFO as below. It is important to complete the requested information as much as possible. Please make sure to leave your correct contactdetails, so we can immediately contact you, in case we have questions.

You can also choose to ask a quote from a member directly. If you choose to do this, we advice you to either contact the member in the country where the shipment departs from or contact the member in the country the shipment is destined for. Of course you can also contact the member in your country.
